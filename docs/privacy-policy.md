# Quick Invoice SaaS Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how Quick Invoice SaaS application ("we", "our", "the Service") collects, uses, and protects personal information from users ("you") accessing or using our service, effective April 2, 2026. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and comply with applicable data protection laws including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

## 2. Data Collection
We collect the following categories of personal data:

### 2.1 Account Information
- Full name, email address, and password for account creation and authentication
- Business details (company name, address) for billing and service provision

### 2.2 Invoice Data
- Customer and vendor contact information
- Transaction details (dates, amounts, descriptions)
- File attachments related to invoices (e.g., contracts, receipts)

### 2.3 Payment Information
- Payment method details (processed through third-party payment gateways)

### 2.4 Technical Data
- IP addresses, device information, and browser type
- Cookie identifiers for session management
- Analytics data (usage patterns, feature interactions)

## 3. Use of Data

### 3.1 Legal Bases for Processing
- **GDPR**: Processing is lawful under Article 6(1)(b) for contract fulfillment and (f) for legitimate interests
- **CCPA**: Collected for purposes of providing the Service as described in our Terms of Service

### 3.2 Purposes
- Provision and personalization of the Service
- Billing and subscription management
- Customer support and service improvement
- Security monitoring and fraud prevention
